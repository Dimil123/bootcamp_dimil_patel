Validations:
Data validated for dates of stock inclusion in the index
Assumptions
1. Futures can be traded at clsoing price without any significant impact
2. Restructing is not expected in near term
3. Corporate actions have negligible impact on individual stock weights change.
Risks
1. Impact of corporate action neglected in the calculations due to lack of data availability, which is having significant
impact on weights of individual stocks and may impact on slipage and replication efficiency.
2. Index restructing is not captured in project, which means if new stock is added cost related to adding it and removing 
old stock may impact slipage and replication efficiency.
